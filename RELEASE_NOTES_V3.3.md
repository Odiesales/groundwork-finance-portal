# Groundwork Finance Portal v3.3

## Currency formatting standardization

All dashboard sections now display monetary values with a dollar sign, thousands separators, and two decimal places (`$0,000.00`).

Updated areas include:
- Executive Scorecard chart hover values
- Accounts Receivable chart axes and labels
- Chargebacks chart labels and axes
- Revenue chart labels, axes, averages, KPI comparisons, and narrative insights
- Existing financial tables and KPI cards retain two-decimal currency formatting

Counts, percentages, ages, units, and pounds retain their appropriate non-currency formats.
